Welcome to CSCI Club

According to the 2015 Department of Campus Involvement study, 71% of St. Cloud State students report being involved on campus. National research shows that students who are involved in activities outside of class have better grades, feel more connected to their university/college, and graduate in less time than students who are not involved. At St. Cloud State University we have over 200 student organization with more than 1,300 leadership positions.
